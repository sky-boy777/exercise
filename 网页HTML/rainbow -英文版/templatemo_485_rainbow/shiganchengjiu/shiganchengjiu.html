Major achievements in economic construction         Unswervingly implement the new development concept, resolutely correct the concept of development, change the way of development, development quality and efficiency continue to improve. The GDP rose from 54 trillion yuan to 80 trillion yuan, ranking second in the world and contributing more than 30% to the world economy. Supply-side structural reforms have been advanced, economic structures have been optimized, emerging industries such as the digital economy have flourished, and high-speed rail, roads, bridges, ports, airports and other infrastructure facilities are rapidly advancing. The modernization of agriculture is advancing steadily, and the grain production capacity has reached 1.2 trillion catties. The urbanization rate increased by 1.2 percentage points annually, and more than 80 million people transferred from agriculture became urban residents. The coordination of regional development, the construction of "Belt and Road", the coordinated development of Beijing, Tianjin and Hebei, and the development of the Yangtze River Economic Belt have achieved remarkable results. The strategy of innovation-driven development has been implemented vigorously, and great scientific and technological achievements, such as innovative national construction, Tiangong, Jiaolong, Tiangyan, Wukong, Mozi and large aircraft, have emerged one after another. Construction of islands and reefs in the South China Sea has been actively promoted. The new open economy system is gradually improved, foreign trade, foreign investment and foreign exchange reserves are among the highest in the world. Major breakthroughs in deepening Reform in an All-round way         Move steadily to advance the overall deepening of reform, resolutely eliminate the shortcomings of various institutional mechanisms. Reform has been carried out in an all-round way, through many points of breakthrough, in depth, with efforts to enhance the systematization, integrity and synergy of the reform, to expand the breadth and depth of reform, and to launch more than 1500 reform initiatives. Breakthrough progress has been made in the reform of important areas and key links, and the main framework of reform in major areas has been basically established. The socialist system with Chinese characteristics has become more perfect, the modernization level of the national governance system and governance capacity has been significantly improved, and the vitality of the development and innovation of the whole society has been greatly enhanced.

Major steps forward in the Construction of Democracy and the Rule of Law         To actively develop socialist democratic politics and promote the overall rule of law, the leadership of the party, the people as masters of the country, the organic and unified system of governing the country according to law has been comprehensively strengthened, the party's leadership system and mechanism have been continuously improved, and socialist democracy has continued to develop. Inner-party democracy is more extensive, socialist consultative democracy has been fully developed, patriotic United front has consolidated and developed, and ethnic and religious work has been innovated and advanced. Scientific legislation, strict law enforcement, just administration of justice, law abiding by the law of the whole people, the country ruled by law, the government ruled by law and the construction of society under the rule of law promote each other. The experimental reform of the state supervision system has achieved actual results, the reform of the administrative system, the reform of the judicial system, the restriction of the operation of power and the effective implementation of the construction of the supervisory system. Great progress has been made in ideological and cultural construction         Strengthening the Party's leadership in ideological work, promoting the Party's theoretical innovation in an all-round way, and Marxism's guiding position in the ideological field is even more clear.Socialism with Chinese characteristics and Chinese Dream are deeply rooted in people's hearts. Socialist core values and Chinese excellent traditional culture are widely promoted, and mass spiritual civilization creation activities are carried out in a solid manner. The level of public cultural service has been improved, literary and artistic creation has continued to flourish, cultural undertakings and cultural industries have flourished, Internet construction and management have been continuously improved, and national fitness and competitive sports have developed in an all-round way. The main melody is louder, the positive energy is stronger, the cultural self-confidence is displayed, the soft power of the national culture and the influence of the Chinese culture are greatly enhanced, and the unity and unity of the whole party and the whole society are more consolidated.

People's living standards are improving         Deeply implement the idea of people-centered development, a large number of measures to benefit the people have been implemented, the people's sense of gain has been significantly enhanced. Decisive progress was made in the battle against poverty, with more than 60 million people steadily lifted out of poverty, and the incidence of poverty dropped from 10.2% to less than 4%. Education has developed in an all-round way, and education in the central and western regions and in rural areas has been significantly strengthened. Employment continued to improve, with more than 13 million new jobs per year in cities and towns. Urban and rural residents' income growth rate exceeded economic growth, and middle-income groups continued to expand. The social security system covering urban and rural residents was basically established, the level of people's health and medical care improved substantially, and the construction of affordable housing progressed steadily. The social governance system is more perfect, the overall social situation remains stable, and national security is comprehensively strengthened. The Party spirit is stronger. Implementation of the new era of good cadre standards, selection and employment situation and atmosphere has improved significantly. The reform of the Party's construction system has been advanced in depth, and the system of laws and regulations within the Party has been continuously improved. We should put discipline ahead of us and work hard to solve outstanding problems that are most strongly reflected by the masses and most threatening the Party's ruling foundation. The system of ecological civilization was formed rapidly, the system of main functional areas was gradually improved, and the national park system was actively promoted.
