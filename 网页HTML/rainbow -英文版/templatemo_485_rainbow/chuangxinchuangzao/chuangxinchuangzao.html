        The vast number of young people must have the courage to innovate and create. Innovation is the soul of national progress, the inexhaustible source of a country's prosperity and development, and the deepest national endowment of the Chinese nation. Life has never been conformable, satisfied with the status quo, never waiting for the enterprising, enjoying its success, but more opportunities for the good and innovative people. Youth is the most energetic and creative group in society, and should be in the forefront of innovation and creation.

        The vast number of young people should have the courage to be the first, to emancipate the mind, to keep pace with the times, to dare to search up and down, to forge ahead, to set up the ambition of surpassing the predecessors' ambitions on the basis of inheriting the predecessors, and to "use my youth as my own, and create a youthful country," The nation of youth ". Must have every mountain to open the road, meets the river to build the bridge will, for the innovation creation but indomitable, moves forward bravely. We should have the attitude of exploring truth, seeking truth and pragmatism, accumulating experience and achieving results in the innovation and creation based on our position.

        Youth is the hope of the country and nation, innovation is the soul of social progress, entrepreneurship is an important way to promote economic and social development and improve people's livelihood. Young students are full of imagination and creativity, and are the fresh force of innovation and entrepreneurship.

It is hoped that the vast number of young students will closely combine their pursuit of life with the development and progress of the country, the great practice of the people, study hard, keep their feet on the ground, forge ahead, and display their talents and serve the society in the process of innovation and entrepreneurship. 5. The future always belongs to young people. Having a large number of innovative young talents is the source of national innovation and the hope of scientific and technological development.

        The vast number of young scientific and technological talents should set up scientific spirit, cultivate innovative thinking, tap innovation potential, improve innovation ability, and continue to surmount on the basis of inheriting the predecessors.

        The vast number of young people should conscientiously devote their youth and contribute more to the building of a well-off society in an all-round way. Youth is very valuable, to be used to do business, hard work, for the future to leave precious memories. The vast number of rural youth should display a new image of modern farmers in the development of modern agriculture and the building of a new socialist countryside, and young people in enterprises should create more wealth in their active participation in productive labour, product development, and management innovation. The vast number of young people in scientific research institutions should delve deeply into knowledge, take the initiative to overcome difficult problems and make more innovative achievements, and the young people of the vast number of organs and institutions should make contributions in improving their level of service to society and the public.

        Young people should keep their calves unafraid of tigers, learn if they do not understand, and work hard to create conditions without conditions. "the trend of the spirit is far away from the sea, and it cannot be limited." Dare to do what you want to have sex, try from scratch, grow up, and make your dream come true. We should dare to be pioneers, not passers-by, as visitors, let innovation become the driving force of youth voyage, let entrepreneurship become the energy of youth fight, and let youth years radiate brilliantly in the service of our country and people.
