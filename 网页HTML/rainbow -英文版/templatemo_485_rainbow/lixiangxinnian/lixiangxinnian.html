Ideal and belief

Everyone has ideals and pursuits, and dreams of their own. Now, everyone is talking about Chinese Dream, and I think that realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is the greatest dream of the Chinese nation in modern times. This dream embodies the long-cherished aspirations of several generations of Chinese people, embodies the overall interests of the Chinese nation and the Chinese people, and is the common expectation of every Chinese people. History tells us that everyone's future and destiny are closely linked to the fate of the nation and the nation. Only when the country is good and the nation is good will we all be better off. To realize the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is a glorious and arduous undertaking, which requires generations of Chinese people to work together for it. The young are the richest and the richest of dreams. Since modern times, the beautiful dream pursued by our youth has always been closely related to the historical process of rejuvenating China. In the revolutionary war years, the vast number of young people full of revolutionary ideals, to fight for national independence, the people's liberation, throwing blood. During the period of socialist revolution and construction, the broad masses of young people responded to the Party's call to march into difficulties, to the wasteland, to defend the motherland, to build the motherland, to work hard and to work hard in the vast world of new China. In the new period of the history of reform and opening up, the broad masses of young people are United to rejuvenate the Chinese nation, blaze new trails and forge ahead for the prosperity and strength of the motherland.


China dream

The ideal guides the life direction, the faith decides the career success or failure. Lack of ideals and beliefs can lead to mental "calcium deficiency". Chinese Dream is the common ideal of the people of all ethnic groups in the country, but also the lofty ideal that the younger generation should firmly establish. Socialism with Chinese characteristics is the correct way to realize Chinese Dream, which our Party leads the people to find after thousands of hardships, and it is also the life faith that the broad masses of young people should firmly establish. Looking forward to the future, our young generation will certainly have a great deal to do, also will have a lot to do. This is the historical law of "the Yangtze River behind waves pushing forward waves" and the youth responsibility of "generation is stronger than generation." The vast number of young people should bravely shoulder the important task entrusted by the times, be ambitious and down-to-earth, and strive to release their youth in the vivid practice of realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation, Chinese Dream.梦想。
