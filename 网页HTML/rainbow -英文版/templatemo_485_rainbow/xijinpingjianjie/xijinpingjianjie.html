  • Main deeds

    Xi Jinping gives important instructions to Comrade Huang Danian's Advanced deeds

    Xi Jinping, general secretary of the CPC Central Committee, president of the State and chairman of the Central military Commission, recently issued an important directive to Comrade Huang Danian on his advanced deeds. Comrade Huang Danian held the ideal of serving the country with science and technology, made the motherland prosperous and strong, and rejuvenated the nation. As the pursuit of life, the people's contribution to happiness has made outstanding contributions to the cause of education and scientific research in our country. Xi Jinping stressed that we should follow the example of Comrade Huang Danian, learn from his patriotic feelings of serving the country in good faith, learn from his spirit of teaching and educating others, and learn from his noble spirit of indifference to fame and fortune, and willingness to devote himself. Integrating patriotism and the will to serve the country into the great cause of the reform and development of the motherland, into the great struggle of the people to create history, starting with themselves and starting from their posts, in order to achieve the goal of "two hundred years". Chinese Dream, the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation, has contributed wisdom and strength. Comrade Huang Danian, a famous geophysicist, was a professor and doctoral supervisor in the School of Earth Prospecting Science and Technology, Jilin University. In 2009, Comrade Huang Danian resolutely abandoned the favorable conditions abroad to return to the motherland, assiduously studying and innovating. Has made a series of major scientific and technological achievements, filled a number of domestic technological gaps, this January 8, unfortunately died of illness, only 58 years old. Beijing, 25 May (Xinhua) (responsibilities: Jiang Pingping, Cheng Hongyi)

  • Ma'am, Peng Liyuan

    Born November 20, 1962, Yuncheng County, Shandong Province, singer and actor

    China's famous soprano, China's first master's degree in national vocal music, a first-grade national actor, enjoys the "government subsidy" issued by the State Council. He joined the Communist Party of China in July 1985. He is a civilian cadre of the Chinese people's Liberation Army, and is treated at the military level. Peng Liyuan was elected for the eighth and ninth consecutive terms, Member of the 10th CPPCC National Committee of the Chinese people's political Consultative Conference. He was elected vice chairman of the All-China Youth Federation in 2005. In 2009, he became head of the Song and Dance Troupe of the General political Department of the Chinese people's Liberation Army. In 2012, he was promoted to president of the PLA Academy of Art. Peng Liyuan is currently president of the Chinese Academy of Art, director of the Chinese musicians Association, vice chairman of the Chinese Opera Research Association, executive committee of the All-China Women's Federation, visiting professor of the Chinese Conservatory of Music, Peking University and Shanghai normal University.
