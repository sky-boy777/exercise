  • development history

  • Taking Advanced Scientific Theory as the Party's guiding ideology

    Since its birth, the Communist Party of China has always taken advanced scientific theories as its guiding ideology.

  • 1921

    In 1921, "Marxism-Leninism" was regarded as the guiding ideology of the Party by the first Congress of the Communist Party of China, and "Mao Zedong thought" was established as the guiding ideology of the Party by the Seventh Congress of the Communist Party of China in 1945 and was written into the Party Constitution.

  • 1945

    In 1945 , the Communist Party of China established Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding ideology of the Party and wrote into the Party Constitution

  • 1997

    In 1997, the 15th National Congress of the Communist Party of China established "Deng Xiaoping Theory" as the guiding ideology of the Party and wrote it into the Constitution of the Party.

  • 2002

    In 2002, the 16th CPC National Congress established the important thought of "three represents" as the guiding ideology of the Party and wrote it into the Constitution of the Party.

  • 2007

    The 17th National Congress of the Communist Party of China wrote "Scientific Development View" into the Constitution of the Communist Party of China in 2007

  • 2012

    The "Scientific Outlook on Development" was established as the guiding ideology of the Party at the 18th CPC Congress in 2012

  • 2017

    In October 2017, the 19th CPC General "Xi Jinping New era Socialism with Chinese characteristics" was written into the Party Constitution.
